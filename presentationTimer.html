How to use

  1. Set "Presentation" time in minutes (default 5 min)
  2. Set "Questions and Answers" time in minutes (default 3 min)
  3. Then: Total session time = Presentation + QandA
  4. Choose sound alarm: Bell, Voice, or Mute
  5. "Start"
    1. Decremental time is shown in blue up to "Presentation time" - 1min
    2. One cowbell sound or voice alarm(one more minute) will be heard and decremental time is shown in orange for one minutes
    3. Two cowbel sounds or voice alarm(end of the talk) will be heard and incremental time is shown in green
    4. Three cowbel sounds or voice alarm(end of session, thank you) will be heard
  6. "Pause" to pause the timer
  7. "Reset" to reset the timer. Presentation and Q and A minutes are kept for subsequent use until the application is reloaded.
Cache will be effective on most of web browsers.
